I am creating this site to reflect on what I have learned and my interests as a developer or musician. Perhaps I’ll make a separate section for hobby related material. In part, this is meant to be a record of where I was at one time and how much progress I have made. I fully expect and intend for this site and its content to change. I’m not an expert at writing or coding, so not everything will be perfect. As time goes on, I want to be diligent about posting even though I am not one who usually shares thoughts publically. I figure that it will be a good exercise in communication even if no one reads anything. 

In a few words: reflection, growth, consistency.

Ok, see ya next time.

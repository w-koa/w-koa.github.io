# About Me as a Developer
Though I am a classically trained musician, I always had the desire to work closely with technology. An avid video gamer, I also enjoy keeping informed about the latest and greatest electronics (that I cannot afford). After teaching myself some basic python I was eager to know more, so I went to bootcamp and learned full stack Java development. Even though I went through an intense course, I know I have barely scratched the surface of what I can do with code and want to continue learning. Now, with great excitement and some nerves, I am on the search to begin my career in software development.

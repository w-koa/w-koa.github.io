# About me as a Developer
As a classically trained musician, we are grounded in the past and but I always had technology in the back of my brain. I have always been a video gamer and enjoy keeping informed about the latest and greatest electronics (that I cannot afford). After teaching myself some basic python I wanted to know more, so I went to bootcamp and learned full stack Java development. Even though I went through an intense course, I know that I have barely scratched the surface of what one can do with code and want to continue learning. Now, with great excitement and some nerves, I am on the search to begin my career in software development.
